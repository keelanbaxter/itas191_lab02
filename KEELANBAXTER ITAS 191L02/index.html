Details about places

Piper's Lagoon

Without a doubt one of the best seats in the Nanaimo to watch a full moon, or blood moon rise. Excellent opportunities for photography, wedding photos, and couples photos. This location hosts two small islands with a trail leading to them both. Benches are provided along the walking path.

Neck Point

Romantic views on the ocean, with an additional view of Vancouver in the distance. This location is pristine for wedding pictures, and for photographers to take advantage of. Neck Point is home to beautiful wildlife such as deer, bunnies, bald eagles, and more! Discover it for yourself at Neck Point today.

Chapters Bookstore

Envelope yourself in knowledge, Starbucks Coffee, and gifts galore with Chapters Bookstore. This is located in North Nanaimo, and serves as one of the most popular bookstores in Nanaimo BC. Come by for a cute bookstore and coffee date all in one, or a quick semi-formal business meeting in the outside patio. Chapters has a lot to offer for gift shopping at Christmas time as well, and is great for birthday gift ideas.

Woodgrove Mall

Climb or claw your way to the top of this great mountain located in the heart of Nanaimo. The mountain itself is like a beacon for those seeking a worthy challenge when it comes to cardio, or just want to enjoy a fantastic view of the entirety of Nanaimo's coastline. From the top, you can clearly see Nanoose Bay, Nanaimo, and Vancouver. Reaching the top itself is a fantastic feeling, and taking a selfie in front of the Canadian flag posted on top of the mountain is a tribute to your time spent in Nanaimo.

Mount Benson

Enter into a peaceful feeling mall. One that reminds of a temple for its ambient natural lighting, decorations, and warm atmosphere. Equipped with a foodcourt, many of the most popular retail stores, and more - including a Wal-Mart attachment. This is the place to be for Christmas time. Come see Santa and his elves, or the local carnival when it comes to town. Nanaimo mall serves as a hub for many things, even as a meeting location. As it is home to the main bus stop.
